Group rules from the admins

1
Food topics and help only, please.
This group is for food topics, homemade meals on a budget and cheap meal suggestions. Helping each other with food ideas, list or suggestions.
2
Review posting rules
Post containing pictures or videos must include a recipe AND full list of ingredients. We are a public group, if you share something it must be public also. Post of very broad categories may not be allowed, ie medical, picky or weight loss. See announcements for full list.
3
Be Kind, Courteous, Positive and/or Helpful.
We're all in this together to create a welcoming environment. Let's treat everyone with respect. Negative comments, rude feedback, feed to animals comments and negative reactions will not be tolerated, that includes GIFs and reactions. Everyone should feel welcome to post their food choices. Please if needed keep scrolling or don't comment.
4
Admin or Moderator Team Choice
Not all post or comment are a good fit for the group. If it is declined, please review the feedback provided.
5
Links, Spam and Personal Promotion not allowed
We have received feedback that members do not want to click a link to go to a site to get the recipe or video. Please include the information in the post. Links to recipes are okay in comments.
6
No Financial Support Request or Offers
This group is here to offer or find meal solutions ONLY. Please work within what someone has listed/"on hand" or the budget they have to spend. We may not allow a dollar amount in a post if it could lead to financial offers. NO Offers or Request for financial support will be allowed. We do not know someone's true situation. This is the internet, be safe, we do not want anyone to be scammed.
7
No Food Shaming or Trash it Comments
If you put down someone's food with a gif, emoji or comment, you will be banned with no warning. This includes "Throw it away" (in any form or combination), puke emojis or gifs that depict the same things. If you can not add helpful suggestions, scroll past it.
8
No Hate Speech or Bullying
Make sure everyone feels safe. Bullying of any kind isn't allowed, and degrading comments about things like race, religion, culture, sexual orientation, gender or identity will not be tolerated.
